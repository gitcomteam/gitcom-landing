Why us?

Right now it's hard for developers to get paid for their open source work, that's where GitCom comes in.
Here's what we offer:

For users

  • We provide an easy way to support open source and independent developers and get rewards in return which can be used later to buy software

For developers

  • Multiple funding solutions such as subscription, selling their products (open core model) and donations
  • By providing a new revenue sources for independent developers they can be more dedicated to their projects thus increasing the overall quality of the open source products.
  • Also, we're actively promoting the platform and all available software thus you can get more traffic for your products

Core Features

For maintainers, we're providing multiple ways to get financial support for their open source projects including Open-core model, selling subscription or support and so on

Open Source to masses


Our goal is to provide a simple way for people to engage with open source software, support it and be a part of its community.
You can support Open Source through subscription, donations or contributions, that's up to you.

Full transparency


People can see & control how their money will be spent and can be truly involved in the future of their favorite projects.

Contribution opportunities, for everyone!

We believe that each contribution should be rewarded so in our platform everyone gets paid based on their effort, from development to testing.

Building on top of existing platforms

We're not trying to replace current open source platforms, we're trying to build on top of them.
We already have integrations with GitHub and GitLab with more coming soon!

We're completely open source

GitCom and all our services are 100% open source. Anyone can view and edit our source code which helps us be more open and secure
